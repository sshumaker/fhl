## Purpose
GitLab develops a security and privacy program that governs a documented, risk-based program that encompasses appropriate security and privacy principles to address all applicable statutory, regulatory and contractual obligations and measures success to ensure ongoing leadership management and risk managment. 

## Scope
All policies and standards having a direct impact to how GitLab carries out it's IT/Security practices are in-scope for this control. 

## Ownership
The owner of this control is Security Compliance.
