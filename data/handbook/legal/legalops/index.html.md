## Overview
The Legal Operations Team is responsible for supporting Legal Team processes with a focus on defining and driving initiatives that improve the team’s efficiency and effectiveness.

Check out the [Legal Operations Portfolio](https://gitlab.com/groups/gitlab-com/-/epics/1641) to learn more about our active projects.

## Procurement
For information on GitLab’s Procurement Team, policies, and processes, visit the [GitLab Procurement Team handbook](https://about.gitlab.com/handbook/finance/procurement/).

Use the [Procurement Guide: Collaborating with GitLab Legal](https://about.gitlab.com/handbook/legal/procurement-guide-collaborating-with-gitlab-legal/) to learn about how Legal assists and interacts with the procurement of products and services.
